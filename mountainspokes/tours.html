Mountain Bike Tours

Montana High County

Enjoy the views that earned Montana its "Big Sky" nickname. Pedal through sacred native ground as you tackle the Continental Divide on Chief Joseph Pass. Soak up plenty of Lewis and Clark history, including the headwaters of the Missouri River. Ride the gorgeous Pioneer Mountains Scenic Byway through the ghost towns and abandoned mines of the Pioneer Mountain Range. Recharge in beautifully scenic lodges as you experience genuine Montana country along the eastern edge of the Rocky Mountain Range.

Includes all breakfasts, 5 lunches, 4 dinners, and lodging at Helena Great Northern, Sacajawea Hotel, and Montana High Country Guest Lodge

Start / Finish
Helena, Montana

Duration
6 days - Sunday to Friday

Levels
Intermediate to Advanced

Dates
August 26. September 2, 2018

Cost: $2,300

Wyoming Bike Tour

Buckle your toe clips, cowboys and cowgirls. Here comes the Wild, Wild West! Ride through lands where coal mines, gold rushes and gun battles defined the landscape and legendary turf wars were won and lost. From Cody, Wyoming, bicycle west into Bighorn National Forest, northeast through Shoshone and Custer National Forests, then back again to Cody. Bike the first protected national forest in the United States. Keep your eyes peeled for the country's largest population of bighorn sheep. Explore the lands of the Shoshone, the Arapaho, the Sioux, Chief Joseph's Nez Perce - and the early settlers pushing west.

Includes all breakfasts, 6 lunches, 5 dinners, and lodging at Helena Great Northern, Sacajawea Hotel, and Montana High Country Guest Lodge

Start / Finish
Cody, Wyoming

Duration
7 days - Saturday to Friday

Levels
Intermediate to Advanced

Dates
August 26. September 2, 2018

Cost: $3,000
