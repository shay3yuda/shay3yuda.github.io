Mountain Bike Tours

Montana High County

Enjoy scenic Montana as you pedal through best vistas this region as to offer. On this tour you will conquer the Continental Divide on the Chief Joseph Pass. Learn your American history has you pass through a ghost town on the edge of the Rocky Mountain Range. Follow in Lewis and Clark's footsteps as you explore head waters of the Missouri River and Mountains Scenic Byway on your week long journey.

Includes all breakfasts, 5 lunches, 4 dinners, and lodging at Helena Great Northern, Sacajawea Hotel, and Montana High Country Guest Lodge.

Start / Finish
Helena, Montana

Duration
6 days - Sunday to Friday

Levels
Intermediate to Advanced

Dates
August 26 - September 2, 2018

Cost: $2,300

Wyoming Bike Tour

Ride the trails of the Wild, Wild West! The land is dotted with coal mines from the gold rush era. See the battle fields of the historical turf wars.

This tour takes you from Cody, Wyoming, to Bighorn National Forest, then northeast through Shoshone and Custer National Forest, and finally back again to Cody. Along the trail, keep a look out for bighorn sheep. Explore ghost towns and learn about the Shoshone, Arapho, and Sioux people.

Includes all breakfasts, 6 lunches, 5 dinners, and lodging at Helena Great Northern, Sacajawea Hotel, and Montana High Country Guest Lodge.

Start / Finish
Cody, Wyoming

Duration
7 days - Saturday to Friday

Levels
Intermediate to Advanced

Dates
Jun 23 - June 29, 2018

Cost: $3,000
