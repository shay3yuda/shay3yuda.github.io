Mountain Bike Tours

MONTANA HIGH COUNTRY

Start / Finish
Helena, Montana

Duration
6 days - Sunday to Friday

Levels
Intermediate to Advanced

Dates
August 26. September 2, 2018

Cost: $2,352

Enjoy the views that earned Montana its "Big Sky" nickname. Pedal through sacred native ground as you tackle the Continental Divide on Chief Joseph Pass. Soak up plenty of Lewis and Clark history, including the headwaters of the Missouri River. Ride the gorgeous Pioneer Mountains Scenic Byway through the ghost towns and abandoned mines of the Pioneer Mountain Range. Recharge in beautifully scenic lodges as you experience genuine Montana country along the eastern edge of the Rocky Mountain Range.

Includes all breakfasts, 5 lunches, 4 dinners, and lodging at Helena Great Northern, Sacajawea Hotel, and Montana High Country Guest Lodge
